Introduction

Welcome to PNU CVLab (Prof. Sangryul Jeon) at Pusan National University.

Our primary research area is Computer Vision. Computer Vision is a core research field in artificial intelligence that investigates computational methods for perceiving and understanding visual data. In addition to Computer Vision, our research also encompasses image signal processing and machine learning, with a special focus on AI-based analysis and interpretation of visual data.

We are particularly interested in both visual data generation and editing, and advanced recognition tasks within Computer Vision.

We are actively recruiting students who are motivated to pursue cutting-edge research.
🪧 If you’re interested, please contact us at srjeonn@pusan.ac.kr.
