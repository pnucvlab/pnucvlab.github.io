Welcome to PNU CVLab (Prof. Sangryul Jeon) at Pusan National University.
Our primary research area is Computer Vision, a field of artificial intelligence that studies how computers perceive and interpret visual information from the real world.
The lab focuses on computer vision, image signal processing, and machine learning, with a particular emphasis on AI-based analysis and understanding of visual data.
We interested in visual data generation and editing as well as advanced recognition tasks.
We are actively recruiting students who are motivated to pursue cutting-edge research in computer vision.
